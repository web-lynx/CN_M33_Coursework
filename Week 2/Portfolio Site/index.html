Alex's Journey at Code Nation

About Me

Hi! I'm Alexander Rayner Wayland, an aspiring Junior Software Developer, based in the North-West of England.

Why Code Nation?

After being made redundant at the start of 2020, I decided that long-held plans to retrain and change my career direction had waited long enough. I was lucky enough to find a social media post by one of the Code Nation admissions team, and after a quick induction, began my first course - Develop: Coding the very next Monday. Since then I've also completed Develop: Cyber and I am now starting the Master: Coding course.
